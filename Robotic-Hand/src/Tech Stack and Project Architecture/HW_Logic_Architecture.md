## Finger and Wrist WIRE / PIN logic

**Purpose:**  
Easier understanding of the project hardware & a simple way to keep track of available / used parts.

_Little finger_
WIRE: white
PIN: 23

_Ring finger_
WIRE: yellow
PIN: 22

_Middle finger_
WIRE: brown
PIN: 21

_Index finger_
WIRE: purple
PIN: 19

_Thumb finger_
WIRE: green
PIN: 18

_Wrist_
WIRE: blue
PIN: 17
